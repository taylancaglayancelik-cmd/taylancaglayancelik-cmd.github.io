Welcome to Zeytin Catalköy

Since our opening in Leyton, we've dedicated ourselves to bringing the authentic taste of Cyprus and the Mediterranean to London. Every dish is prepared with care, using traditional recipes and the finest fresh ingredients.

Whether you're joining us for a casual meal or a special occasion, we invite you to experience the warmth of our restaurant and the passion behind every plate.

Our Story

Zeytin Catalköy was born from a simple idea: to share the culinary heritage passed down through generations. Led by our head chef Sakaria Ali, we've built a restaurant where tradition and passion come together on every plate.

Our name honors our roots, and our dishes honor the authentic recipes of Cyprus and beyond. We believe in quality over quantity, freshness over convenience, and genuine hospitality above all else.

Our Specialties

From our signature Lahmacun and tender Kebabs to the grilled perfection of Hellim cheese, each dish represents the best of Mediterranean cuisine. Our menu celebrates authentic flavors—expect no shortcuts, no frozen ingredients, and no compromises.

Visit our menu page to explore our full selection of dishes, each one prepared fresh to order.

Popular Favorites

Our most-loved dishes include the crispy Cigara Borek filled with cheese and fresh herbs, grilled Hellim with its perfectly chewy center, and our authentic Turkish Delight for a sweet finish. Each dish tells a story of tradition and craftsmanship.
