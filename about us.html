Our Story

Zeytin Catalköy was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that food should tell a story. Our name, Zeytin, means olive in Turkish—a symbol of the Mediterranean's richness, health, and tradition. Catalköy represents the villages where our recipes originated, passed down through generations of families who cooked with love and integrity.

What started as a passion for sharing authentic cuisine has grown into a thriving restaurant trusted by the community. We've stayed true to our roots while opening our doors to new neighbors and friends, one plate at a time.

Our Philosophy

Authenticity Matters. We don't cut corners. Every ingredient is chosen carefully, every recipe honored as it was meant to be prepared. We cook the way our families have cooked for centuries—with fresh produce, bold spices, and time-honored techniques.

Community First. We believe a restaurant is more than a business; it's a gathering place. Our customers become our friends, and we remember their names, their preferences, their stories. We take pride in being part of the neighborhood.

Quality Over Convenience. We choose to do things the right way, not the fast way. This means longer prep times, smaller batches, and dishes made to order. But that's what creates the difference you taste in every bite.

Meet Our Head Chef

Sakaria Ali is the heart of Zeytin Catalköy. With decades of culinary experience and a lifelong passion for Mediterranean cooking, Sakaria brings his heritage and expertise to every plate. For him, cooking isn't a job—it's a calling.

"When you eat here, you're not just getting food," Sakaria says. "You're getting a piece of tradition, a piece of our family, and most importantly, care in every detail. That's what I promise to every customer who walks through our doors."

Sakaria leads a dedicated team committed to maintaining the highest standards of flavor, freshness, and authenticity.

What We Believe In

  • Fresh Ingredients: We source the best produce and quality meats from trusted suppliers who share our commitment to excellence.
  • Traditional Methods: Ancient recipes deserve traditional execution. We honor time-tested cooking techniques passed down through generations.
  • Customer Care: Every guest is important. We listen, we adapt, and we go the extra mile to ensure your experience is memorable.
  • Community Responsibility: We're proud to be part of the neighborhoods we serve and committed to supporting our local community.
  • Dietary Inclusivity: We understand dietary needs matter, and we're happy to accommodate vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free requirements.

Our Growing Family

What began as our flagship restaurant in Leyton has grown to include locations in Shoreditch, Covent Garden, and Hackney. But no matter which restaurant you visit, you'll find the same commitment to authenticity, quality, and hospitality that defines Zeytin Catalköy.

Each location carries the same values: treat customers like family, honor our traditions, and never compromise on quality.
